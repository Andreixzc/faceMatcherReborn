Project Overview

This project aims to provide photographers with a convenient platform to filter their photos efficiently, particularly based on specific individuals with the use of a convolutional neural network (CNN)

What is the application?

The application was developed with the aim of assisting photographers in filtering their respective photos based on the selection of a specific person.

How can I use the application?

To use it, simply create an account on the website, create a folder, and upload your photos.

After that, upload the reference person from which you want to filter the images and click on the "find matches" button.

What are the highlights of this application?

This application consumes a robust and scalable API developed by me and fully hosted on the Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure.

Furthermore, even though it is in beta version, it is completely free and easily accessible to the general public.
